Venus of the Arts


Arman's Venus of the Arts is a unique work of art. Consisting of several elements. It transcends its form by incorporating a copy of Venus de Milo and a Benin bronze representing a africain woman, a traditional African work.

The composition of the face gives off a strange and unhealthy side that provokes in his reader a strange attraction for the work.
